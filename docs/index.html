How it works

Designed to get out of your way

Clock-first home screen

Your home screen shows only the time. No feeds, no notifications, no infinite scroll. Just clarity.

App allow-listing

Choose only the apps that deserve your attention. Everything else stays hidden — accessible, but not tempting.

Distraction blocker

Set focus sessions and lock yourself out of distracting apps. Your future self will thank you.

Screen time insights

Simple, honest stats about your daily usage. No shame — just awareness to help you improve gradually.

Light & dark modes

A grayscale palette keeps the UI calm and intentionally understimulating — beautiful in any light.

Minimal widget support

Add only what matters — a task, the weather, a quote. No clutter. Every pixel earns its place.
